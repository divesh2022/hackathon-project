Tech Stack - Rural Health Helpline

Backend:
- Python (FastAPI) or Node.js (Express)
- PostgreSQL (Normalized schema)
- Twilio/BSNL for voice & SMS
- AI: Symptom classifier, OCR, Image recognition, NLP

Frontend:
- React.js (Doctor/Admin dashboard)
- Android (ASHA app, offline support)

Infra:
- AWS EC2 + S3 (Cloud hosting & storage)
- JWT Auth
- Monitoring: Grafana

AI/NLP:
- Whisper (voice-to-text)
- IndicBERT (multilingual)
- Tesseract (OCR)

Security:
- AES encryption
- Role-based access
- Compliance with Indian IT & Health Data laws
